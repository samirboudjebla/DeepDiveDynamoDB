About Pinehead Records

Pinehead Records was founded in 1977 by brothers John and George Pine. They operate New York's most popular record shop in Manhattan's trendy East Village. Pinehead Records has grown over time to become a NYC landmark. Known as a cultural destination and rare-vinyl mecca for devoted music lovers and collectors, it originally opened as a used bookstore, and expanded in 1995 to become what it is today. Pinehead Records carry second hand and some limited new merchandise and collectors' items. Music genres range from Classical, Rock, Jazz, to Avant-Garde on 45s and LP records. Pinehead's collection is impressive for both its breadth and depth — it’s the uncommon kind of shop where you can easily walk out with a bag of painfully obscure soul 45s and out-of-print metal albums as well as low-priced '70s classics and the latest from that buzzworthy band your friend just blogged about.

Pinehead Records boasts a collection of over 100,000 vinyl albums in stock.
